Milk Tea

Okinawa milk tea is a popular milk tea flavor inspired by Okinawa, Japan. It’s known for its rich, creamy taste combined with a slightly caramelized sugar syrup called brown sugar or black sugar syrup. This gives the drink a deep, sweet flavor with hints of caramel and molasses, making it smooth and comforting. It’s often enjoyed with chewy tapioca pearls, balancing sweetness and texture perfectly!.
